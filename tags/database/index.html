The ultimate AeroPress technique

Mar 16, 2017
Having made countless cups of AeroPress’d coffee (mostly at work), I’ve finally arrived at the perfect cuppa. Boil the jug. Yup, boil. You’ll see why in a moment. Pour boiling water into your coffee cup. If you take milk in your coffee leave room for milk. Otherwise fill it up. Grind coffee / put ground coffee in an inverted AeroPress. Fill AeroPress with hot water from cup. Your cup is now hot and the water has cooled to the perfect temperature. ☞
